History

Thammasat University

Thammasat University is a leading Asian academic institution, with its world-class standard in producing graduates, creating bodies of knowledge and addressing the country’s issues with commitment to virtues and the public interests.

"Thammasat University follows its guiding philosophy to uphold academic excellence, services to society, promotion of arts and culture, social equality and social justice under the philosophies of constitution and democracy.”

Associate Professor Gasinee WitoonchartRector of Thammasat University

THA PRACHAN CAMPUS

Thammasat University Tha Prachan Campus offers lectures in Undergraduate Programs which offer international and special programs in Social Sciences and Humanities, such as the Faculty of Law, Faculty of Commerce and Accountancy, Faculty of Political Science, Faculty of Economics, Faculty of Liberal Arts, Faculty of Journalism and Mass Communication, College of Interdisciplinary Studies, College of Innovation, and Pridi Banomyong International College (PBIC)

Location: Thammasat University, 2 Prachan Road, Phra Barom Maha Ratchawang, Phra Nakhon, Bangkok 10200
Tel: 0-2613-3333, 0-2221-6171-80, 0-2221-6111-20

RANGSIT CAMPUS

LAMPANG CAMPUS

Lampang Campus is situated about 15 kilometers from town, covering the area of 364 rai. Its first complex is named “Sirindhararat Building”. It currently offers courses in the Faculty of Law, College of Interdisciplinary Studies, Faculty of Social Administration, Faculty of Fine and Applied Arts, Faculty of Public Health, and Faculty of Science and Technology.

LAMPANG CAMPUS
Location: Thammasat University Lampang Campus 248 Moo 2 Lampang-Chiang Mai Road, Pongyankok, Hangchat, Lampang 52190
Tel: 0-5423-7999
Fax: 0-5423-7999 ext. 5119

Our graduate degree programs

Our graduate degree programs offer courses in all fields, covering sociology, humanities, science and technology, and health sciences, in Thai, bilingual, and international programs. There are two types of lecture sessions: normal programs are conducted in office hours and special programs in the evenings or on weekends.
